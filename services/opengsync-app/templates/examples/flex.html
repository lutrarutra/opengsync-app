Columns
  • Sample Name:
    • Name of the previously specified Flex sample.
  • Multiplexing Pool:
    • Which pool the sample belongs to for multiplexing.
    • 1 Multiplexing Pool = 1 Gem Reaction = 1 Flex Library
  • Barcode ID:
    • E.g. 'BC001'
    • If sample is multiplexed with two or more barcodes, separate them with a semicolon (e.g., 'BC001;BC002').
